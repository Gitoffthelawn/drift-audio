# Credits

Drift bundles audio recordings from several sources under open licences. This
file lists the attribution for each layer that ships in the app. In-app credits
(generated from the live catalogue) are also available from the menu, so the two
stay in step.

All recordings have been edited for use in Drift — segmented, loop-baked, EQ'd,
compressed, and loudness-normalised. This processing is noted to comply with
Creative Commons change-disclosure requirements.

---

## Synthesised layers

Forest, Propulsion, Warp, and Radio Chatter are scaffolded in the catalogue but
ship without audio yet; they arrive with a later synthesis pass. When added they
will be original work under Drift's MIT licence, or will carry their own source
attribution here if built from recordings.
